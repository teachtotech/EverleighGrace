Our Story

It all started with some bath bombs...

Everleigh Grace began as a small side business for Amber Dunn to earn some extra money as she stayed home with her children. What started out as a spa and beauty business quickly turned into a boutique cottage bakery in the heart of the heartland, Duncan, Oklahoma.

Now, Amber perfects her craft of small batch, hand crafted desserts and pasteries serving all of southwest Oklahoma. Her goods are enjoyed far and wide by all, young and old.

Our Goals

At Everleigh Grace, we aim to create one united family by providing desserts to celebrate all milestones, big and small. From weddings to babyshowers, birthdays to promotions, we are here to cheer you on and provide a sugar rush all at the same time. We can't wait to grow with you!
